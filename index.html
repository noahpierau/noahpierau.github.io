About me:

I ask questions and search for solutions.

As a consultant I have worked 5+ years with organizations in areas such as sustainability, spirituality and blockchain technology. While working I have gained extensive experience with online community building and I developed a passion for story telling and content creation.


I believe in the following core values:


Good thoughts

Our thoughts have great power. With that power comes great responsibility. Everything vibrates at a certain frequency and so does our brain. We are in constant communication with everything around us. Therefore, if we intend to change the world in a positive way, we need to become aware of our thoughts. Which thoughts do we have? What do we send into the world?

Good words

Words are manifestations of thoughts. Once spoken, they can be building blocks to construct a positive vibe, or they can be bricks that bring people down. Words also affect our self-image and how we experience reality. The way we tell our story will determine how we feel about ourselves, or about a certain situation. Stories can change the world. Which words will we use?

Good deeds

Action is essential. Without deeds, our thoughts and words are empty. There are familiar expressions "what goes around comes around" and "you reap what you sow" that relate to the law of Karma. Everyone is responsible for what they do in life. For every action, there is a reaction. What are we doing with our lives? Are our actions in line with our feelings, thoughts and words?

Finding my ikigai

Ikigai is a Japanese concept meaning "reason for being"
